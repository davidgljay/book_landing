What We Do

Movement Strategy

How might your organization strategically invest in relationship? Who might you inviting into relationship and how might you prepare for unpredictable but strategically relevant results? How is your organization already part of larger movements that share your values, and how might active participation in those movements further your mission?

Relational Operations

Once you know who you want to invite into relationship how do you create the conditions for those relationships to flourish? This almost always requires an ecology of relational containers: large tentpole events interspersed with smaller more frequent gatherings. How do you build a robust ecology with limited resources?
